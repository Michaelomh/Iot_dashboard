Shopping Cart Overview Dashboard Information & Instructions
This project was created for my IS439 Internet of Things module. For the project, our group used embedded devices on shopping trolleys in hopes that it would reduce the amount of shopping trolley misuse. The primary user of this dashboard is an enforcement officer supervisor, to give him a mini control center to have real time analytics on what is going on in Jurong Point.
